Atleti gear up to host Betis

Our team takes on Betis at the Wanda Metropolitano on Saturday. The game is to kick off at 9pm CET.

Atleti return to LaLiga action on Saturday as we host Betis at 9pm CET.

Simeone’s men arrive at the fixture as the only side that remains unbeaten in the league. After our 2-1 win at Balaídos, we’ll look to earn our second straight win in LaLiga and add three points to the eight we’ve picked up in the four games we’ve played so far.

Betis, on the other hand, are seventh with nine points in six matches. They lost to leaders Real Sociedad in their last match, but they also have a fantastic away record – having won two of their three games on the road.

The last time both sides met at the Wanda Metropolitano was on July 11. Our team won 1-0 on the night thanks to a Diego Costa header.
