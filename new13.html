Simeone: “I liked our team’s attitude, we tried to score until the end”

Our coach and Herrera reacted to our defeat in Munich.

Simeone reacted to Wednesday’s 4-0 defeat against Bayern Munich.

“This result hurts, but there are important things we can take away for the future. I liked our team’s attitude. We tried to score until the end and could have done so if we’d been more clinical. We had two or three chances in the first half and two or three in the second. My feeling is that we worked very well,” our coach said.

Herrera also shared his thoughts on the game.

“We played very well against the best team in the world, probably. We had our chances, but we didn’t take them. They were clinical and they punished us. We never stopped fighting and now we have to keep working hard,” the Mexican said.
