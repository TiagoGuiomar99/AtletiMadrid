The stats for Atleti-Salzburg

Our team takes on the Austrian side at the Wanda Metropolitano on Tuesday. The game is to kick off at 9pm CET.

Atleti gear up to host Salzburg at 9pm CET on Tuesday.

- Our team are yet to lose against an Austrian club (seven wins and one draw in eight matches).

- In those eight games, we’ve scored 21 goals and conceded six.

- Salzburg have a record of four wins, two draws and eight defeats against Spanish clubs, netting 13 times and conceding 22.

- The Austrian club are participating for the third time in the Champions League group stage, although they qualified 13 other times for the competition.

- Atleti have only lost once in 15 European games at the Wanda Metropolitano. We’ve kept 11 clean sheets in the process.

- Dominik Szoboszlai was the player that attempted the most crosses (nine) on matchday 1 of the Champions League as the Austrians faced Lokomotiv Moscow.

- Lucas Torreira (10 out of 10) and Thomas Lemar (seven out of seven) completed every pass they attempted on matchday 1.
