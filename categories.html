Categories and Modalities

There are different CATEGORIES AND MODALITIES depending on the age of the holder.


The main novelty for the 20/21 season is the creation of a new Youth category, with a reduced fee for young Atléticos that are from 14 to 24 years old, as well as the extension of the age group of the Child Membership to 13 years old.


As a rule, the criteria used is the age you turn during the 20/21 season. For example, if you turn 14 in August 2020, you will be qualified to apply for the Youth Membership for the entire season that you are 14 and would therefore have youth status.


For membership card purposes, the season is considered to run from 1 June 2020 to 31 May 2021.

  • BENJAMIN MEMBERSHIP (from 0 to 5 years old)

  • Born after 01/06/2015.

  • Annual fee: 29 euros. It's only paid when you register for the first time and is free for the following seasons.

  • CHILD MEMBERSHIP (from 6 to 13 years old).

  • Born between 1/06/2007 and 31/05/2015.

  • Annual fee: 29 euros

  • YOUTH MEMBERSHIP (from 14 to 24 years old).

  • Born between 1/06/1996 and 31/05/2007.

  • Annual fee: 43 euros

  • ADULT MEMBERSHIP (over 25 years old).

  • Born before 1/06/1996.

  • Annual fee: 58 euros


As a club member, if you want to attend matches at the Wanda Metropolitano on a regular basis and always have the same seat reserved, you can add the type of season ticket that best suits your preferences: LaLiga pass, full pass, mid-season pass, parking pass or any other type of ticket offered.


Therefore, there are two MEMBERSHIP OPTIONS


  • NON-SEASON TICKET HOLDER

  • Club members who don't have a season ticket for the matches played at the Wanda Metropolitano. These are usually Atlético fans who live far away from Madrid, even abroad, and therefore cannot usually attend home matches. In other cases, they're also Atleti fans who, due to their personal circumstances, are unable to attend matches frequently. They will only pay their annual membership fee.

  • SEASON TICKET HOLDER

  • Club members who, in addition to their affiliation to Atleti, want to attend the matches held at the Wanda Metropolitano on a regular basis and purchase a season ticket so that they always have the same seat reserved at the stadium. In addition to the membership fee, they apply for a season ticket of their preference. Click here for the types of season tickets available for the 20/21 season.
