Atleti look to keep streak going at demanding El Sadar

Our team faces Osasuna on the road at 6.30pm CET on Saturday.

Atleti are ready for a new demanding challenge on the road as we visit Osasuna’s El Sadar on Saturday. The game is to kick off at 6.30pm CET.

Simeone’s men will look to keep their positive streak going following two straight wins – against Betis and Salzburg – and breaking our record of straight undefeated LaLiga matches. The tally currently stands at 21. In our last away game, we beat Celta 2-0 thanks to the strikes from Suárez and Carrasco.

Osasuna, on the other hand, are seventh in the league standings with 10 points – their best start in a decade to a LaLiga campaign after six matches. Moreover, Jagoba Arrasate’s side have won two of their three home fixtures.

We last played at El Sadar on June 17 and we earned a big 5-0 win.
